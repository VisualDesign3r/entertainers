HUMAN NATURE

×

Phil Burton

    Show: Human Nature Sings Motown & More

    Location: The Venetian Resort / Sands Showroom

    Years in show: 10

    Home town: Sydney, Australia

Background training/education: All of us have had singing lessons at various times in our life, as well as a few dance lessons here and there, but nothing formal. The closest thing to formal instruction we received was being part of a school-based entertainment training workshop called the “Talent Development Project” which gave talented young performers a basic grounding in things such as stagecraft, repertoire choices and other facets of live performance. Passions outside of work: Golf - the trick is finding time to pursue it! Favorite things to do in Vegas: Having a glass of wine at the front bar of Mon Ami Gabi. It’s great for people and fountain watching, and you don’t have to wait ages for a balcony table. Most memorable/satisfying moment in the show: Most of our audience have never heard of us or what we do before they see the show, so it’s a nightly challenge for us to convert these people into fans. It’s always a wonderful moment when you can sense the crowd are all on your side, and are really having a great time.
